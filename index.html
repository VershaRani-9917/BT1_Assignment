Welcome to the World of BTS

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010. The band consists of Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook, who co-write or co-produce much of their material. Bangtan Sonyeondan in Korean means "bulletproof boy scouts." Koreans call them by this name, however people in other countries refer to them as BTS.

Explore their journey frome early days to becoming global superstars. Learn about their Members, their chart-topping discography, and their memorable tours. BTS has redefined K-pop and pop culture, and this website is your guide to their amazing world.
